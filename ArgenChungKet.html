Update 18/12/2022 13:12 CST

Hello everybody and welcome along to our live blog for the World Cup final, as Argentina and France square off at Lusail Stadium, in Qatar. They're going for the biggest prize in international football and we'll be covering it all right here for you.

The referee for this World Cup final is Szymon Marciniak (POL). The two assistant referees will be Pawe Sokolnicki (POL) and Tomasz Listkiewicz (POL).
